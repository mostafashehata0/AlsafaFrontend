About Us

With over 30 years of experience, ALSAFA has managed to secure its position as one of the market leaders in telecommunication networks. Pairing the experience acquired over the years, with dedicated and specialized engineers, known to be of the bestcaliber, has always been ALSAFA's key to success. ALSAFA has also worked on enhancing its internal capabilities by forming in-house teams of splicing and air blowing technicians, and supplying them with the latest and best-in-class machines in order to ensure the efficiency and success of the projects delivered. In efforts of ensuring that every aspect of the telecommunications networks is covered, ALSAFA has created its own factory to manufacture manholes, allowing us to be self-sufficient in covering the requirements of our ongoing projects
